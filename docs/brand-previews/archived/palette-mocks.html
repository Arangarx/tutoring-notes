Mynk — palette comparison
brand-walkthrough phase 2 · 2026-05-19

Three concepts, same content.

Each column renders the same five surfaces in one of the three candidate palettes from docs/MYNK-BRAND-PHASE-2-DECISIONS.md. Toggle light/dark at the top to compare both modes simultaneously.

The surfaces are: landing hero · dashboard · live recording strip · AI-generated field treatment · mascot placeholder (geometric/minimalist register, not a final sketch). Mock copy uses the agreed B-voice with D-moments framing — landing tag leans D, dashboard/recording strip lean B.

Pay attention to: (1) does this feel like a tutoring product or a financial product? (2) does the accent earn its keep as a product affordance — does "AI-generated" actually read as AI-generated? (3) at a glance, which one would a department head trust within 5 seconds?
