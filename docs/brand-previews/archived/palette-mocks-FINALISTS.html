Mynk — finalists
10 contenders for the family table · 2026-05-19

Ten finalists, all in one place, all rendered against the same five mock surfaces.

This is the consolidation doc — everyone (you, your wife, the daughter) can sit in front of one screen and rank, instead of toggling between four mock pages. Each card includes its hex codes, light + dark modes, and a "vs the other finalists" note explaining what this one does that no other finalist does.

The matrix below rates each on four axes, plus flagged risks. Toggle light/dark in the header. Cards are larger than on the earlier pages so the typography and surface hierarchy read more honestly.
