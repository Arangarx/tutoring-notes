Mynk — warm-direction palettes
6 candidates for the daughter · 2026-05-19
For the daughter

Warm-direction palettes — six options, with "Fiery Ocean" up front.

"Fiery Ocean" is the one you specifically asked about (first column, with the coral ring around it). It's actually a warm-meets-cool palette — deep red + cream + navy — so I also pulled five neighbors that play in the same warm territory, ranging from "all warm" to "warm anchored by something cool."

Toggle light/dark in the header. Each column shows the same five surfaces (homepage, dashboard, recording bar, AI-generated field, mink mascot). Hex codes are on every swatch.

Heads up for dad: these are noticeably louder than the cool/considered palettes in round 2. Your wife already said "yellow, yuck" on Concept 1 — palettes 2 (Warm Autumn Glow) and 6 (Sunny Beach Day) lean yellow/gold, so flag those as exploration-only.

The one you asked about. Not pure warm — it's deep red + cream + navy in a 2-warm-1-cool tension. That's why it's called "Fiery Ocean": the navy keeps it from feeling alarmist, the red keeps it from feeling cold. Honest reading: the red is in the territory of warning signals (close to Coca-Cola red, close to error states). For a tutoring app where you want students and parents to feel calm, that's a real risk. But the look is genuinely memorable — Stripe red + Linear navy energy.
