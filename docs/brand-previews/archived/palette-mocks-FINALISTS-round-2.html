Mynk — round 2 finalists
3 finalists + MWW light v2 + 4 mashups · 2026-05-19

Round 2: the three finalists, plus four bridges between Concept 3 and Midnight Woodland Whimsy.

You and the daughter narrowed it to Concept 3 (Warm graphite), Midnight Woodland Whimsy, and Mystic Waters, with C3 and MWW out front. Two open questions: (1) MWW isn't warm — can we keep its pleasant outdoor calm but make it feel less indifferent? (2) MWW light mode reads a little washed out — can it be made more confident?

This page answers both. Section 1 reprints the three finalists for reference. Section 2 is MWW with a higher-contrast light mode (dark mode unchanged). Section 3 is four mashups exploring what happens when you blend C3's warm cream + coral pop with MWW's forest-green DNA.

How to read the mashup pedigree strip. Each mashup card has a colored ribbon showing where each token came from — orange = Concept 3, green = MWW. The hex codes on the swatches are the actual values used in that card's light mode (top three) and dark mode (bottom two), so you can copy them into a Figma comp or paste them into someone's chat.

Section 2 · Midnight Woodland Whimsy — light v2 contrast fix · dark unchanged

The diagnosis: original MWW light uses a mid-sage accent (#487D68) on a cream-sage surface (#F2F5EE). They live next to each other in lightness and saturation, so the CTA button and the AI-content tint both feel underpowered. v2 fixes that: keep the same MWW DNA, but use deep forest (#1F3F3A) as the accent for buttons/CTAs, brighten the surface to near-white (#F8FAF3) so green has room to register, and demote sage (#487D68) to a strong-border role instead of a primary one.

What M3 is: Light mode is byte-identical to Concept 3 (cream + teal + coral). Dark mode is byte-identical to MWW (deep navy + sage). What M4 is: Both color families live in the system at once. Teal (#1E4D4A) is the brand (logo, hero, links). vs the others: The most "design system" of the four. You get a teal brand AND a forest support color AND a coral pop, which means more flexibility for sub-features later (e.g., "AI" content could lean forest, "Live recording" could lean coral, brand pages stay teal). The risk: three accent families is one more than a small product needs — could read as undecided rather than rich.
