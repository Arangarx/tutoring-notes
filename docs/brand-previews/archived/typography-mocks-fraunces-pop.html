Mynk — Fraunces pop variants
for the daughter · "B but louder" · 2026-05-19

Six ways to make "B" pop more.

System B from the typography mocks uses Fraunces at weight 500 — restrained on purpose. But Fraunces is a variable font with weight (100–900), optical-size, italic, and the unusual SOFT axis (sharp ↔ rounded). Lots of room to dial up "pop" without changing the family.

What "pop" can mean here, technically: heavier stroke (weight), bigger size (scale), sharper contrast (opsz at display sizes), softer/friendlier forms (SOFT axis), or italic for personality. Each variant below pulls one or two of these levers so you can compare cleanly.
