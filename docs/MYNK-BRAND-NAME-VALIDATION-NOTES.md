## Name origin (founder narrative, captured verbatim 2026-05-18)

Andrew's origin story (paraphrased from chat — full phrasing preserved where exact wording matters):

Started from brainstorming many synonyms around learning, teaching, knowledge transfer (functional naming + pure vibe naming approaches). Wanted a pithy name. Hit on **Mind + Sync** as a portmanteau → contracted to **Mync** → drifted to **Mynk** when the mink mascot synergy emerged. Andrew quote: *"It feels generally stronger and more intentional, it's cute, easy to remember, and with a mink mascot is double win."*

**Brand story implication**: Mynk syncs minds — between tutor and student, between session and parent update, between what was taught and what gets remembered. Mascot adds: small, attentive, curious, fast — a learning companion.

**Pronunciation**: Pronounced exactly like "Mink." Cold-test data:
- 3/3 cold-asked respondents pronounced "Mink"
- Andrew's daughter: *"either Mink or My-nk...probably Mink? Possibly Mihnk"*
- Verdict: strong intuitive spelling-to-sound mapping; rare for stylized respellings (most invented spellings fail this test).

**Alternative considered but deprioritized**: InSession — therapy site already uses it; "InSession" is generic English which is intrinsically weak as a mark even absent the conflict; Andrew not fully divorced but Mynk wins on cute + memorable + multi-word portmanteau + mascot upside.

**May-15 BACKLOG shortlist** (TutorStudio / SessionPilot / Mentivo): disposition DEFERRED to brand walkthrough Phase 2.

## Research findings synthesized (2026-05-18, 4-stream parallel research)

### Deal-breakers cleared

- **No live US trademark** for MYNK in IC 009 (Computer Software) or IC 041 (Education Services). All US MYNK filings dead/abandoned.
- **No live US exact MINK** in IC 009 or IC 041 either. Only adjacent beauty (IC 044) and fashion (Rebecca Minkoff IC 018) marks exist.
- **No tutoring/edtech SaaS competitor** uses Mynk/Mink/Mync/Minc/Mynx/Mynck naming anywhere — confirmed against Wyzant, Outschool, Varsity Tutors, Tutor.com, Skooli, Preply, Cambly, MagicSchool, Notably, Mindgrasp, Brilliant, Khan Academy, Coursera, Udemy, Quizlet, Duolingo, Mighty Networks, Mathnasium, Sylvan, Kumon.
- **No offensive/sexual/religious meaning** in any major language checked (Spanish, French, German, Mandarin, Cantonese, Japanese, Korean, Arabic, Portuguese, Hindi, Russian, Italian, Dutch, Hungarian).
- **SEO bar holds** (Andrew's framing: "surviving 'first search result is another tutoring SaaS with 40k users' is not survivable"). Top SERPs for "mynk" / "mynk tutoring" / "mink tutoring" are noise (VR creators, beauty, fashion, fur, university portal homographs, solo educators) — NO tutoring SaaS dominates.

### Material findings that adjusted the plan

1. **`mynk.com` unobtainable at reasonable cost.** Parked Korean ownership since 2012 (Jaewan Lee, Gwangju KR), listed for sale on Afternic with no public BIN. Realistic broker range mid-4 to low-5 figures. **DECISION (Andrew, 2026-05-18)**: skip acquisition; commit to `usemynk.com` as permanent primary.

2. **`@mynk` handles widely held by small unrelated accounts.** X (@mynk = Mayank Rungta, ~2K followers, freelance tech, unrelated), TikTok (private empty shell), YouTube (Mayank Rai personal channel), GitHub (dormant since 2010), Bluesky (Japanese personal), Mastodon (empty since 2019). No edtech competitor among them. **DECISION (Andrew, 2026-05-18)**: standardize on `usemynk` across all social platforms. **Critical exception**: `linkedin.com/company/mynk` slug is STILL AVAILABLE (404) — grab ASAP, only platform where exact "Mynk" identifier can still be claimed on a major surface.

3. **Strategic complication: Moroccan fintech "Mynk" (QAZI Finance).** Active product at [mynk.ma](https://www.mynk.ma), ~10k+ Android downloads in Morocco/MENA, **owns `mynkapp` on both LinkedIn AND TikTok**. Not a direct competitor (fintech ≠ tutoring) but: (a) kills `mynkapp.com` as viable defensive primary, (b) creates SEO collision on "mynk app" queries, (c) becomes annoying if Mynk ever expands internationally OR adds payments-adjacent features (parent invoicing, tutor payouts). **Mitigation**: pilot scope stays US-focused + tutoring-not-payments lane. `mynkapp.com` + `mynkapp` social handles dropped from grab list entirely.

### Background findings (flagged but not blocking)

- **MYNC (Grass Valley video editing software)** is closest USPTO conflict — live IC 009 mark, Reg. 5520428, incontestability accepted 2024-01-23, registered 2018-07-17. Different goods/channels (pro video post-production vs tutoring SaaS) — manageable but expect possible office action when/if Mynk formally files. Not pilot-stage issue (filing deferred per pre-trademark backlog P4).
- **India MYNK Class 41 VOD mark** (Mangata Technologies Private Limited / Miners Inc / myNK ecosystem) — registered 2019-03-09 valid to 2028-09-10. Matters only for India expansion (not on roadmap).
- **Mink-the-animal cultural noise** (fur industry, mink lash extensions, Rebecca Minkoff fashion, Minky baby blankets) — soft positioning friction, not legal blockage. **Mitigation**: cartoon mink mascot (not photorealistic) distances from fur/lash associations cleanly.
- **Brand-noise non-competitors using Mynk spelling**: Mynk Creations (VR avatars), MYNK Cosmetics, MYNK London band, Monica Mynk (individual tutor/author, monicamynk.com), Mynkis (AI SEO content automation, unrelated category), Mynk Mobility (Indian electrical equipment manufacturing). All low-severity noise.

### International notes

- **Germany / Netherlands / Russia**: locals think "Nerz/nerts/норка" not "Mynk" — pronunciation drift, not blocker. Light touch in international landing copy if expanded.
- **Hungary**: "mink" ≈ "we" (Hungarian first-person plural pronoun) — only real meaning collision in Latin-alphabet markets. Low priority.
- **China/Japan/Korea**: katakana ミンク / Hangul 밍크 transliterations work cleanly. Latin "Mynk" works as foreign SaaS name (common pattern).
- **Morocco**: fintech-Mynk already owns this territory. Avoid.
- **India**: MYNK Class 41 VOD trademark blocks.

| **Greenlight Mynk?** | YES, for US-only tutoring pilot stage | Deal-breakers all cleared; yellow flags adjusted around, not blocking |
